The era of VR sports

With technological advancement in VR, it did not take long for companies to see the potential of VR Sports. Before VR started, there were already games for Wii, playstation and xbox that involved actual movement of the players like dancing, playing tennis, jumping and more. No that VR is getting more and more popular, VR sport is likely to take off as it can even be used by anyone with a smartphone and affordable vr glasses.

Making fitness fun

One major selling point of VR sports over conventional sport is the gamification in it. There are many people who simply find going to the gym boring and not interesting, but they might enjoy video games and competing with others. VR sports can be a fun way of staying fit and healthy. Whether you enjoy fantasy games, musical challenges or just don't feel like leaving the house to play tennis with your friend in the rain, there is a solution for everyone. Additionally, it can make sporting courses available to people who are not mobile and can't reach a gym or other sporting facilities.

Getting started with VR sports

If you are interested in starting with VR sports, we have all the information you need! To get a feel for it, you migth want to start with getting some affordable VR glasses and try them out with your smartphone. While this is not as interactive as the ones form gaming consoles and meta, it will give you an indication of how VR sports work and if you actually enjoy it. Are you sure that you enjoy VR sports and want real interaction? Try out VR glasses from the gaming consoles or meta. These are of course more expensive, but they create a feeling of being in a completely different world.

Get as fit as a celebrity

Do you want to be as fit as a celebrity? VR sports are also a popular method of celebrities to stay fit! Mark Zuckerberg is of course one of the most famous celebrities who uses it a lot and is working on improvements in that area with the oculus. Other celebrities interested in the advancement of VR include Joe Rogan, Nicole Kidman, Barack Obama, Kevin Spacey, Caysey Neistat and Duncan Trussell.

What does VR sports include

VR sports can include many different things. Some companies offer just the glasses to put your smartphone in while others offer packs with VR glasses with built in screen, a camera that camptures your movements as well as controllers. It really depends on what you are looking for and what your budget is. Either way VR sports includes movement, new perspectives, competing with people all over the world and hopefully a lot of fun .

What is the future of VR sports

The future offers many possibilities for VR sports. With time the VR sets with cameras and controllers will become more affordable to the common people. Additionally the technology will advance as well, potentially leading to more intertwined virtual worlds and reality. It could also provide professional teams with a new way of training certain tactics and reviewing scens of previous games to improve reactions. This could also help in reducing training-induced injuries, as players train in VR separately and avoid dangerous collisions.
