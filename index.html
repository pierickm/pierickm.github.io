Staying healthy through innovation

Smart watches

Smart watches have been around for a while now and are an incredibly popular choice to keep track of your health and sport achievements. High-quality smart watches, made to track sports and health are a great tool to check your fitness progress while working out and being active and it can remind you of your goals. By now they can be used in extreme environments as well so you can even track your diving session or your ascent of Mount Everest.Buy a smart watch

Performance trackers

Like some smart watches, performance trackers are a great tool to track your sporting progress. However, these were first marketed towards professional athletes and are now made more affordable to amateur athletes. Unlike smart watches, they usually do not have a display. Some of them can be connected to your phone via an app where you can check your data live. Others still have to be connected to a laptop or pc to see the data. These performance trackers come in various forms, they can be a small chip that can be clipped on a shoe or a device that can be put in a pocket. Buy a performance tracker

Virtual reality sports

VR opens up a completely new way of staying fit. Where someone might lack motivation and self-discipline for conventional solo sports like running, swimming or going to the gym. With VR sport, a whole new option emerges. You can experience a gamified version of keeping fit in the comfort of your own home. No need to drive to the gym, run in the rain or drive to a swimming pool. No one else around staring at you, unless of course you choose to sport with others virtually. Buy VR gear here
